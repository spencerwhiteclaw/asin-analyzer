Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

ASIN Analyzer ("we," "our," "us") operates the website asinanalyzer.app. This page explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Information We Collect

When you use ASIN Analyzer, we collect the email address you provide when running an analysis or subscribing to our newsletter. We also collect the ASINs you submit for analysis, along with standard web analytics data such as browser type, IP address, and pages visited. If you make a purchase, payment information is processed directly by Stripe — we never store your credit card details on our servers.

How We Use Your Information

We use your email address to deliver your analysis report, send product updates and marketing communications you can unsubscribe from at any time, and process your purchases. We use ASIN data to generate your listing analysis and to build aggregated, anonymized benchmark data that improves our scoring accuracy for all users. We use analytics data to improve our website and understand how people use our product.

Data We Access From Amazon

ASIN Analyzer accesses only publicly available product listing data from Amazon.com. We do not access your Amazon Seller Central account, and we do not require or store any Amazon credentials. The data we analyze includes product titles, prices, images, bullet points, reviews, ratings, and Best Seller Rank — all of which is publicly visible on any Amazon product page.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only with service providers necessary to operate our business: Stripe for payment processing, our email service provider for delivering reports and communications, and our hosting provider for running the application. All service providers are bound by their own privacy policies and data protection obligations.

Data Retention

We retain your analysis reports and account data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you services. You can request deletion of your data at any time by emailing us. We will delete your personal data within 30 days of a verified request.

Cookies

We use essential cookies for session management and optional analytics cookies to understand site usage. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though some features may not work properly without them.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. You can unsubscribe from marketing emails at any time using the link in any email. To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the email below.

Security

We use industry-standard security measures including encrypted data transmission (HTTPS), encrypted data storage, and secure authentication. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. We'll notify you of significant changes by email or by posting a notice on our website.
