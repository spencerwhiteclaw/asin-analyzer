Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get paid? ▼
Monthly payouts are processed via Rewardful, our affiliate platform partner. You'll receive payments directly to your bank account, PayPal, or other payment methods supported by Rewardful. Payouts happen automatically on the 15th of each month for the previous month's commissions.
What's the cookie duration? ▼
We track referrals for 60 days. If someone clicks your link and signs up within 60 days, you'll earn the commission. This gives you plenty of time for your referrals to decide and convert.
Do I earn on annual plans too? ▼
Yes! You earn 40% of every subscription payment, whether it's monthly or annual. If someone signs up for an annual plan, you get 40% of the entire annual payment upfront, and then 40% of renewals every year.
Can I promote to my own audience? ▼
Absolutely! You can promote ASIN Analyzer to your email list, social media followers, YouTube channel, blog readers, podcast audience, or any other platform where you have an audience. We just ask that you follow our promotional guidelines and be transparent about the affiliate relationship.
Is there a minimum payout? ▼
No minimum! Even if you earn just a few dollars, you'll receive your payout. There are no fees or thresholds to clear. Whatever you earn is yours.
How do I track my referrals? ▼
You'll have access to a dedicated affiliate dashboard where you can see real-time data on your referrals, clicks, conversions, and earnings. You can track everything and optimize your promotion strategy based on what's working.
