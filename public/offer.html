What is A/B testing on Amazon?

Amazon's "Manage Your Experiments" tool lets Brand Registered sellers test two versions of their title, bullets, or images. Amazon splits your traffic 50/50 and tells you which version drives more sales. It's free to use and built into Seller Central. We give you step-by-step instructions to set it up.

Yes, Amazon's Manage Your Experiments requires Brand Registry. If you don't have it, you can still use the optimized title, bullets, and keywords — just swap them in directly instead of A/B testing. You'll still see improvements.
