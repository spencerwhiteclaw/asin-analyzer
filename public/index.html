FAQ

Questions you should be asking.

Is the free report actually free? No catch?+
Yes. Enter your email and ASIN, get a full 12-point diagnosis with scores across all categories and a list of every problem we find. No credit card, no login required. The free report shows you WHAT's wrong — our paid plans show you HOW to fix it with step-by-step implementation plans.
How is this different from Helium 10 or Jungle Scout?+
Those tools are for product research — finding what to sell. ASIN Analyzer is for listing optimization — making what you've already launched convert better. They find products. We fix listings. Complementary, not competitors.
Can I analyze my competitor's listing?+
Absolutely. Any public ASIN works. Smart sellers run their top 5 competitors through the tool to find gaps and vulnerabilities. The Seller plan includes competitor comparison features so you can see scores side by side.
What do I get with the Seller plan that's not in the free report?+
The free report shows you what's broken. The Seller plan ($19/mo) gives you 10 reports per month, implementation plans with step-by-step fix instructions for every issue, score tracking over time so you can see your listing improve, competitor analysis, and PDF exports. It's the difference between knowing the problem and knowing the solution.
What's the difference between Agency and Enterprise?+
Agency ($49/mo) gives you 50 reports per month — same features as Seller, just more volume for bigger catalogs. Enterprise ($199/mo) gives you 300 reports plus keyword rank tracking, automatic monthly re-scans, white-label branded reports, and category intelligence data.
How accurate is the data?+
We pull real-time data directly from Amazon product pages — pricing, reviews, BSR, images, A+ content, Q&A, everything. The scoring algorithm is calibrated against top 1% seller practices.
Where do I find my ASIN?+
It's the 10-character code starting with "B0" in any Amazon product URL. You'll also find it in the "Product Information" section of any listing page. You can also paste the full Amazon URL — we'll extract the ASIN automatically.
Can I cancel anytime?+
Yes. No contracts, no commitments. Cancel in one click from your account settings. 30-day money-back guarantee on all paid plans.
