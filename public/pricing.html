Questions? Answers.

Can I cancel anytime?+

Yes. Cancel any time from your account settings. No contracts, no cancellation fees. If you cancel, you keep access through the end of your billing period.

What counts as a "report"?+

Each ASIN you analyze counts as one report. Re-running the same ASIN counts as a new report (because we pull fresh data each time). Your quota resets on the 1st of every month.

What's the difference between the free report and paid?+

The free report tells you WHAT is wrong — all 12 categories scored with specific findings. Paid plans add the Implementation Plan — step-by-step instructions on HOW to fix every issue, plus keyword priorities, action calendars, and score tracking over time.

Can I upgrade or downgrade later?+

Absolutely. Upgrade any time and the price difference is prorated. Downgrade takes effect at your next billing cycle. Your reports and history are always preserved.

What is Keyword Rank Tracking?+

Enterprise-only feature. We check where your ASIN appears in Amazon search results for up to 50 keywords. You can see if you rank #3 or #300 for "silicone nipple covers" — and track how your ranking changes over time as you optimize. This replaces the need for tools like Helium 10's keyword tracker.

Is my data secure?+

All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We only access publicly available Amazon data. We never access your Seller Central account or store any Amazon credentials.
