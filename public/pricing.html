Questions? Answers.

What is the optimization loop?+

Every report includes AI-optimized titles, bullets, and keywords written for your specific listing. You A/B test them in Amazon's Manage Your Experiments, wait 2 weeks for real data, then come back to re-score and get a new round of optimized copy. Repeat every 2 weeks — each cycle makes your listing stronger.

Do I need Brand Registry for A/B testing?+

Amazon's Manage Your Experiments requires Brand Registry. If you don't have it, you can still use the AI-optimized copy — just swap it in directly instead of A/B testing. You'll still see improvements from better-optimized copy.

Can I cancel anytime?+

Yes. Cancel any time from your account settings. No contracts, no cancellation fees. If you cancel, you keep access through the end of your billing period.

What counts as a "report"?+

Each ASIN you analyze counts as one report. Re-running the same ASIN counts as a new report (because we pull fresh data and generate new optimized copy each time). Your quota resets on the 1st of every month.

What's the difference between the free report and paid?+

The free report shows your 12-point diagnosis — WHAT is wrong. Paid plans add AI-optimized copy (title + bullets rewritten), A/B test instructions, keyword priorities, implementation plans, and score tracking over time. The paid plan tells you HOW to fix it and gives you the actual copy to use.

What about the $7 Implementation Plan?+

The $7 plan is a one-time purchase for a single ASIN — one set of optimized copy, one A/B test cycle. Monthly plans give you the optimization loop: new copy, new tests, new scores, every 2 weeks — across as many ASINs as your plan allows.

Is my data secure?+

All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We only access publicly available Amazon data. We never access your Seller Central account or store any Amazon credentials.
